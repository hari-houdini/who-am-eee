B.E. Electronics and Communication Engineering, Anna University

2014 - 2018

Eight semesters of signals, circuits, systems, and everything in between. From transistor physics to TCP/IP: mathematics, electronic devices, communication theory, digital signal processing, microprocessors, VLSI design, embedded systems, RF and optical communication, wireless networks, and computer architecture

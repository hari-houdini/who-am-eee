B.E. Electronics and Communication Engineering

Anna University

2014 – 2018

Eight semesters of signals, circuits, systems, and everything in between. From transistor physics to Network protocols, wireless embedded systems, and computer architecture.

Computer Networks covered the full OSI stack — not just what HTTP is but why the entire internet is designed the way it is.

With Computer Architecture, I have a deep understanding of how CPUs are actually built: pipelines, cache hierarchies, memory organisation, the hardware decisions that explain why software behaves the way it does.

Microprocessor and Microcontroller meant writing actual assembly for the 8085 and 8086 — opcodes, registers, interrupts, nothing between you and the metal. Trust me, I felt like a hacker writing assembly codes.

Senior Web Engineer

Pixel Toys

2022 – Present

A games studio by identity, a surprisingly serious engineering operation by necessity. The web platform to serve an active user base in the millions globally, the stakes for performance, reliability, and architecture are real, not theoretical. Felt more like Zoho, but the difference is, we get to start from scratch. We decide the tech stack, and the architecture, and then we build it, unlike Zoho, where we get to build on top of a matured stack.

Honestly such an eye opening pahse of my career. Made me realise the difference between web development and engineering. And even if I lack the skills, it provided me a space to proactively learn and contribute to the platform.

The work involves performance engineering and cloud infrastructure ownership, from frontend rendering strategy and bundle optimisation to AWS infrastructure provisioned as code (IaC), monitored end-to-end, and load-tested before anything went near production.

Mentoring junior engineers here was slightly different from people management at Zoho. I was able to understand the distinction between managing people and developing engineers.
